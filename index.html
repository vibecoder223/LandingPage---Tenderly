FAQ

Questions teams ask before switching.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are drafting answers on a real RFP within the first afternoon. Connecting your knowledge base, importing past proposals, and configuring routing typically takes one to two business days.

Where does Tenderly pull answers from?

From your own content. We connect to SharePoint, Google Drive, Confluence, Notion, Highspot, and Seismic, plus any past proposals or policy documents you upload directly. Every generated answer cites its source.

What about confidentiality and security?

Tenderly is SOC 2 Type II certified and ISO 27001 compliant. We support SSO, SAML, audit logging, and customer-managed encryption keys. Your data is not used to train shared models.

Does the AI hallucinate answers?

Tenderly is built to refuse rather than guess. If an answer isn't supported by your knowledge base, it routes to a human reviewer with the gap clearly flagged, rather than inventing a response.

Can we keep our existing templates and tone?

Yes. Tenderly learns your house voice from approved past proposals and applies your brand templates on export. Reviewers see drafts in your tone, not generic AI prose.

How is pricing structured?

Per seat, with volume tiers for organizations responding to large numbers of RFPs each quarter. Enterprise plans include private deployment, custom integrations, and a dedicated solutions engineer.
